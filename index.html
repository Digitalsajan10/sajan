How to Register a Free .com.np Domain in Nepal?

Looking to launch a website or blog in Nepal? Learn how to secure a free .com.np domain with these detailed steps and requirements.

Obtaining a free .com.np domain in Nepal is a great opportunity for individuals, businesses, and organizations looking to establish an online presence. Here's a comprehensive guide on how to register:

  1. Review the Terms and Conditions carefully to understand the guidelines and requirements for registering a .com.np domain.
  2. Ensure proper configuration of your domain by reviewing the provided "Name server configuration example."
  3. Follow these steps for the official request:
    1. Check the availability of your desired domain name by entering it and clicking "Search."
    2. If available, proceed to click "Register Now" to begin the process.
    3. Create an account by providing your email and desired password.
    4. Fill out the registration form with accurate details.
    5. Verify your email and sign in to your new account.
    6. Attach the required documents, ensuring they meet the specified criteria.
    7. Submit your request and wait for approval.
  4. For local entities, required documents include:
    • Certificate of registration of company/firm/partnership /organization.
  5. For foreign entities registered in Nepal, required documents include:
    • Certificate of registration of branch or liaison office, or
    • Certificate of affiliation with Social Welfare Council.
  6. For non-incorporated entities doing business in Nepal, such as Permanent Establishments or Non-incorporated Joint Venture Entities, the required document is a PAN registration certificate.
  7. For entities (local or foreign) with business interests in Nepal, required documents include:
    • Certificate of trademark registration in Nepal, or
    • Official filing receipt of trademark application issued by the Department of Industry (to be supported with actual trademark registration certificate within 3 years of domain registration).
  8. For personal requests, required documents include:
    • Nepalese citizenship, passport, driving license, voter's card, or
    • Non-Resident-Nepalese ID Card, or
    • Nepalese resident visa for foreign nationals.
  9. Ensure that personal domain names are based only on the applicant's name as mentioned in the provided documents.
